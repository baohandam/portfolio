Hi, I'm Bao!

I'm an aspiring UX/UI Designer.

Although I'm just starting out, I already bring nearly two years of experience in both the tech industry and consulting. I adapt my skills to meet the needs of any client, delivering high-quality, tailored solutions with fresh ideas and a versatile approach to ensure every project's success.

I'm based in Berlin but originally from a small town near Würzburg, Bavaria, where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Human-Computer Systems in March 2024. My passion for creativity started in childhood, evolving from drawing to working with textiles, various DIY projects and exploring the musical field.

Growing up between two cultures and in a lower-income family has shaped my unique perspective on life. I deeply value empathy and kindness, and I believe that promoting inclusion and accessibility allows us all to contribute to a more just and equitable world.
