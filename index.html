How I work

What drives my design

01

Understand before designing

I dig into the real problem first. The best design decisions come from truly understanding context — users, business goals, and constraints.

02

Abstract → Concrete

PMs and stakeholders often have vague visions. I ask the right questions and shape fuzzy ideas into clear, testable designs and flows.

03

Value over aesthetics

Pretty is a bonus. I'm focused on building products that actually work — for real people, in real situations, accessible to everyone.

About me

Hi, I'm Bao!

I'm a UX/UI Designer based in Berlin with nearly two years of experience in tech and consulting. I have a Bachelor's in Human-Computer Systems and a genuine love for making complex things feel simple.

I grew up between two cultures and that shaped how I see things — I care deeply about empathy, inclusion, and making products that work for everyone, not just the loudest voice in the room.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably sewing something, making music, or working on a random DIY project. Creativity is just how I exist.
